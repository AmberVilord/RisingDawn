How It Works

Circadian Fragrance (TM) is crafted to evolve with your morning, not just evaporate and disappear. Lighter notes lift first for a bright opening, while richer molecules stay closer to skin and unfold as your body naturally warms. The result is a smoother diffusion curve: a softer start, a gradual bloom, and a grounded finish that holds steady instead of dropping off. It is a formula-led progression that feels synchronized with your energy - from pear dew clarity, to soft florals, to warm amber and skin musk.

Phase One

Pear dew and cold air clarity spark the first inhale.

Phase Two

Soft florals lift as energy rises through the morning.

Phase Three

Warm amber and skin musk ground the day in confidence.
