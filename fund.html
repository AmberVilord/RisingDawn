The Rising at Dawn Fund for Women

When you begin your day with intention, you help another woman begin her future.

The Rising at Dawn Fund for Women is a proposed initiative designed to support women who are pursuing education, returning to school, or launching their first business. Inspired by the quiet power of morning rituals, this fund honors the unseen work women do to build new chapters of their lives.

This is not charity as an afterthought.

It is purpose designed into the experience.

Behind every sunrise is a woman choosing to rise.

Some rise before their children wake. Some rise before their first class of the day. Some rise before opening the doors to the business they are brave enough to build. The Rising at Dawn Fund for Women exists to support those moments of becoming.

Why This Fund Exists

It is dedicated to women who carry ambition and responsibility side by side, and who understand that growth often happens in the quietest hours. The fund supports those moments of becoming, when a decision to rise is also a decision to invest in the future.

How the Fund Works

A portion of each conceptual purchase contributes:

  • $3 from every full-size Rising at Dawn Eau de Parfum
  • $1 from every body care product
  • $5 from every full Rising at Dawn ritual set

Each ritual is a personal moment. Each purchase is a shared investment in another woman’s future.

Funds are designed to support:

  • Tuition and certification programs
  • Childcare assistance for student mothers
  • Laptops and learning resources
  • Micro-grants for women launching small businesses

Our Annual Impact Vision

The Rising at Dawn Fund for Women aims to support at least 25 women annually through educational scholarships and small business startup grants. Each recipient represents a new beginning, a renewed sense of possibility, and a future shaped by intention.

This vision reflects a belief that luxury can be both beautiful and purposeful. That innovation and care can coexist. That a brand can elevate experience while expanding access to opportunity.
